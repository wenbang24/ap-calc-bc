$$
A=\int_a^b(f(x)-g(x))\,dx
$$
if $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ are continuous and $f(x)\geq g(x)$ on $[a, b]$.
# Functions of x
$$
A=\int_a^b(\text{top curve}-\text{bottom curve})\,dx
$$
# Functions of y
$$
A=\int_c^d(\text{right curve}-\text{left curve})\,dy
$$
